Chapter 2.1

Updated User Journey Map

The new updated journey map for Lydian money follows Jennifer, 27 and always buys coffee from her favourite coffee shop.

However, today she’s forgotten her wallet at home and it often frustrates her when she realises she forgets something important.

Luckily for Jennifer, she recently signed up to use Lydian Money, a mobile app that allows her to use FaceID to verify her credentials, and uses mobile payments to pay for her morning coffee.

On this particular morning, Jennifer didn’t realise that her phone bill and subscriptions were charged to her card, and after paying for her coffee, notices that her budget circle is red. While this stresses her out for a moment, she quickly realises she can double check what went wrong, and goes to the spending screen to view her recent transactions.

After realising the culprit, she remembers that it is the beginning of a new month, which means pay day! Immediately, she goes to the track screen to check her bank balance. Additionally, every month she reviews her budget, to see if she’s been spending appropriately. While she’s still waiting for her coffee, Jennifer can continue to use the same app to view her budget and can clearly see that her spending is still within the budget she set last month!
