Chapter 1.2

Succinct summary of the problem we are attempting to solve

Australians find dealing with their finances to be stressful, overwhelming and frustrating. Also, complicated and siloed financial systems and their management tools require users to juggle several different systems at a time, making financial management increasingly difficult and stressful.

The Banking Royal Commission also shed a light on the financial literacy gap that revealed some consumers were paying more in fees than the interest on their accounts. However, everyone has a different financial goals and knowledge, which makes it hard to suggest a clear solution that suits for everyone.

To help ease financial stress and management situation, tools that help make your financial activity more transparent are required to reduce the stress induced by ever change finances. Because everyone has different financial goals and management style, it is also necessary to build a tailored financial app that helps users to manage their finances all in one place.
