Chapter 1.3

Design Concepts: Benefits and Drawbacks

As part of task 2, we were required to come up with 3 different design concepts, each drawn upon the research gathered from task 1 to determine solutions through the reframing model.




The mobile application is a one-stop financial management app that helps one manage their financial accounts, budgets and mobile payments. It gives tailored managing finance solutions, which allows users to easily track their spending. It provides a more transparent financial history and report on money spending patterns. However, it can be challenging for this app to grow with the user together. As one’s financial goal is changing it also depends on what stage of life they are currently in; the app needs to suit every need.

Concept 2

Financial Companion

Finance Companion is developed to reduce the stress originating from managing personal finances. A traffic light system through three indicating lights, red, yellow and green show what financial situation a user may currently be in and also shows basic bank account information and one’s financial summary on the screen. The lights indicate sudden changes that may occur during the uncertainty of your financial future. Starting and finishing the day with a financial report is efficient for those who need constant reminders about their budget. However, it needs more environmental context and it lacks depth beyond being a physical budgeting device, limiting its potential and development.




The interactive bank experience is built as an external experience for customers at a bank. When a customer arrives, they are greeted with the familiar digital kiosk that provides them with a ticket but after this, transforms into a new experience. In this financial game, the customer is quizzed on certain financial content, and presented with a final score at the end. This idea differentiates itself by existing not as an app or home device, but as part of a new digital transformation at your local bank. Unfortunately, this idea was not pursued due to its fairly limited environment setting, as well as its low replayability potential.
