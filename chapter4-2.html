Chapter 4.2

Video Walkthrough

For our promotional video, we began by asking, how can we make an app exciting? As we were looking around at other videe advertisement examples, it was clear that there is a current trend in the mobile industry for fast paced, high energy promos.
We essentially broke down the industry formula into 3 different elements

1. The music, something fast, with a regular beat
2. Short, sharp and direct language, whether it be through animated text or spoken word
3. Show the audience what they should be looking at. Time cuts, zooms or slides to the beat, utilising the lines of movement to direct the audience's eye

This is the formula that essentially made up the promo you can view below.
